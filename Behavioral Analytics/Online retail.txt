
 ---<Customer Behavior Analytics>---

Dataset Link: https://data.world/uci/online-retail

This is a descriptive analysis aims to uncover trends 
and behavioral patterns to promote and expand the bussiness
the dataset taken is transactional data which contains 
transaction records in between 01/12/2010 and 09/12/2011 
for a UK-based and registered non-store online retail.
The company mainly sells unique all-occasion gifts. 
Many customers of the company are wholesalers.

The tool used for data manupulation is MYSQL workbench
and Tableau for data analysis and Visualiation.
the procedure for that is importing dataset>Data Wrangling
>Data Analysis>Data Visualization>Sharing.
